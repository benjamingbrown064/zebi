═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
   HOW IT WORKS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

OBJECTIVE CREATION:
1. User: "Reach £50k MRR by June"
2. AI: Analyzes company context
3. AI: Generates 3-6 milestones
4. AI: Creates 2-5 projects
5. AI: Creates 10-20 tasks
6. Result: Full breakdown in 10-30 seconds

DAILY MONITORING (Automatic):
1. Calculate progress (velocity, rate)
2. Assess trajectory (on_track/at_risk/blocked)
3. Detect blockers (AI analysis)
4. Generate action plan if needed
5. Auto-create tasks to resolve issues
6. Update status & alert if needed

CROSS-ENTITY INTELLIGENCE:
AI can reference:
- Objective data (current, target, timeline)
- Company profile (industry, stage, revenue)
- Company memories (learnings)
- Company insights (strategic analysis)
- Projects, tasks, velocity
- Progress history (30 days)
- Active/past blockers
- Dependencies
