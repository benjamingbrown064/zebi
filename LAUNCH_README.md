## 🚨 Risk Management

### Early Warning Signals (Monitor Daily)
- Critical bug count increasing
- Product completion % stalling
- Lead generation velocity slowing
- Beta customer engagement dropping

### Escalation Path
1. **Minor issues:** Handle in daily standup
2. **Medium blockers:** Flag in weekly review
3. **Critical blockers:** Immediate escalation to Ben

### Red Flags (Immediate Escalation)
- Critical bug in production
- Product completion <85% by March 28
- <20 qualified leads by March 28

- Any objective >7 days behind schedule
